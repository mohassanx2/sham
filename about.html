About Me

Meet the Instructor: Sham Bassam Barhoumeh

Jewelry Designer

I’m Sham Bassam Barhoumeh, a jewelry designer and certified trainer with over eight years of experience in the world of fine jewelry design. I combine artistic vision with technical expertise in 3D modeling and visual storytelling to create meaningful, timeless pieces. Through my certified training programs, I’ve guided more than 200 students to build their own creative paths in jewelry design. My mission is to help aspiring designers turn their ideas into real, market-ready creations with creativity, precision, and passion.

Our Mission

"To craft timeless, emotionally resonant jewelry that transforms personal stories into artful heirlooms, empowering individuals to express identity, love, and transformation."

Our Vision

"To pioneer a movement of emotional design in the Arab world, where jewelry becomes a soulful language bridging heritage, innovation, and personal truth."
